Bio

Andrew Leifer is a graduate of Stanford University with degrees in Physics, Political Science and interdisciplinary honors in International Security Studies. Andrew is currently a PhD candidate in the Biophysics program at Harvard University. He conducts research in the Samuel Lab where he uses new optogenetic techniques to probe the neurocircuitry of the brain. Most everyone calls him Andy. This website serves as a repository of Andy's writings and works. Feel free to contact him at:

leifer@fas.harvard.edu
